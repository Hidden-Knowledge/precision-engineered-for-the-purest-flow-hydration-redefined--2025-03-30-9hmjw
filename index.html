Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to replace the filters?

Our smart monitoring system will notify you when it's time to replace filters, typically every 6-12 months depending on usage. The app provides real-time filter status updates.

Is installation complicated?

Not at all! Most customers complete the installation in under 30 minutes. We provide detailed instructions and video guides, plus our support team is available 24/7 if you need assistance.

What minerals are retained in the filtered water?

Our system maintains beneficial minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium while removing harmful contaminants. The mineral balance is optimized for taste and health benefits.

What's covered under the warranty?

We offer a comprehensive 5-year warranty covering all system components. This includes free replacement parts and technical support throughout the warranty period.
